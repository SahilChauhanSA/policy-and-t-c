Data Deletion Instructions

If you have used the "Avsar" app and want to delete your data associated with your Facebook login, follow the instructions below:

  1. Go to your Facebook account settings.
  2. Navigate to Settings & Privacy > Settings > Apps and Websites.
  3. Find Avsar in the list of connected apps.
  4. Click Remove to delete all data shared with our app.
